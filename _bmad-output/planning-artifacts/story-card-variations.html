Design Notes

/polish

Subtle shadows, refined transitions, header divider, priority dot glow. Premium feel.

/colorize

Left border accent, orange project name, saturated status, avatar ring in priority color.

/delight

Bouncy hover, chevron affordance, pulse animation, rounded corners, larger priority dot.

/simplify

No avatar (initials only), no priority dot, minimal padding, inline compact layout.

/bolder

Strong borders, box-shadow hover effect, pill-shaped priority, high contrast text.

/normalize

Current baseline — unified structure, consistent spacing, Heroicons, priority dot.
