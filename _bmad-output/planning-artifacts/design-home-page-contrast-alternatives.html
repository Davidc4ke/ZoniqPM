Recommendation

Approach E (Combined) offers the best balance: subtle off-white container (#FAFAF9) provides enough contrast against white cards without being visually heavy. The colored top-border creates instant container identity without overwhelming the cards. The white cards pop against the slightly darker container while maintaining the clean aesthetic.

Alternative F is good if you prefer the card color to vary by container type (warm gray for assigned, blue tint for review)—this creates stronger semantic association.
